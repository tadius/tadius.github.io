This Privacy Policy explains how Compare Price ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

We do not require users to create an account and do not collect personal information such as name, address, or phone number.

All in-app purchases are processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

Learn more: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us.
